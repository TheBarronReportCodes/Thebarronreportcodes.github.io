About

Just like every other 90s kid, I loved watching Yu-Gh-Oh, Dragonball Z, and my favorite, Pokemon. I guess my Dad took noticed and bought me my very first game system, a Gameboy Advance. Man did I play that system from sun up to sun down. Catching Pokemon, Summoning monsters, Instant transmission. I always dreamed of one day becoming the person to create that game or impact it in some way.

You see, every great breakthrough in society has involved a strive for creativity. From your phone that can recognize your voice to that book you’re so engrossed in that it keeps you up all night, it wouldn’t exist without someone having the thought, rather the incessant hunger, to create. I love to create things, whether it being for me or someone else. To impact humanity in some beneficial way and past down what I've learned to the next generation, that is my goal.
